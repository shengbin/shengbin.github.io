IPGateway

IPGateway is a simple but useful app for logging in/out of the IP gateway of Peking University. Choose the version you need.

IPGateway-iOS

This is IPGateway for iPhone and iPad.

It is available in the App Store:

https://itunes.apple.com/cn/app/ipgateway/id493761518?l=en&mt=8

By the way, you may also use this client: IPGWClient.

IPGateway-Mac

Click the following link to download the .zip file, extract it, then you will get IPGateway.app(better be put into /Applications folder).

http://pan.baidu.com/share/link?shareid=3482412976&uk=3808900647

IPGateway-Android

Click the following link to download the .apk file, and install it on your Android devices.
